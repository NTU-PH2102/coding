1. Open Chong_Tian_En_02_Code.m

2.Run the matlab File.

3. Enter the Parameter Settings at the command windows. Special options include total number of frames, duration(not simulation duration), force visualisation, outputting animation to gif

Example Command Window:

Enter "d" in metre:1
Enter "B_0" in Tesla:5
Enter "q_0" in Coulomb:1
Enter "m" in kg:1
Enter "V_0" in Volt:0
Enter "A1" for x amplitude:1
Enter "A2" for y amplitude:1
Enter "B" for z amplitude:4
Enter "phi_z" for z initial offset angle in radian:0
Enter "phi_a" for azimuthal initial offset angle in radian:0
Choose w_c+ (0) or w_c- (1):1
Enter total number of frame for simulation:2000
Enter time t in second for simulation:30
Visualise force? Yes(1) No(0):1
Output animation to GIF? Yes(1) No(0):0
